2011 conference

"It's the context, stupid!"

Theme: The theme of the 35th annual conference was taken from the title of a 1994 article by Paul Saffo in Wired magazine. In the article, Saffo wrote "It is not content but context that will matter most a decade or so from now. The scarce resource will not be stuff, but point of view." More than fifteen years later, context has never been so important in providing information to library customers.
More info:  For more information, please visit the 2011 Conference site at http://elag2011.techlib.cz/en/.
